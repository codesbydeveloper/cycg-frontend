Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/01/2026

At CYCG, protecting the privacy and confidentiality of information entrusted to us is a fundamental priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how CYCG collects, uses, stores, and safeguards personal and business information in the course of delivering strategic advisory and consulting services.

By accessing or using our website, services, or engaging with CYCG in any capacity, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

CYCG may collect the following categories of information:

a. Personal and Professional Information
This may include name, job title, organization name, email address, phone number, and other contact or professional details provided through inquiries, engagements, or communications.

b. Business and Organizational Information
formation related to organizational structure, leadership, governance, operations, strategy, or performance that is voluntarily shared in the context of advisory, consulting, or project engagements.

c. Technical and Usage Information
When visiting our website, we may collect non-identifying information such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and interaction data to improve performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected by CYCG is used strictly for legitimate business purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Providing advisory, consulting, and strategic services
  • Responding to inquiries, requests, and service-related communications
  • Improving our website, offerings, and service delivery
  • Complying with legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations

CYCG does not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

3. Confidentiality and Data Protection

CYCG operates in environments where trust, discretion, and confidentiality are critical. We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure.

Client and engagement-related information is treated as confidential and is used solely for the purposes defined within the scope of the engagement.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

CYCG may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who support our operations and are bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Where required by applicable law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of CYCG, our clients, or stakeholders

Any sharing is conducted on a need-to-know basis and aligned with this Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, meet contractual requirements, or comply with legal and regulatory obligations. Once no longer required, information is securely deleted or anonymized where appropriate.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or updating of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of personal information, subject to legal or contractual limitations
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

Requests can be made by contacting CYCG using the details provided in the Contact Information section below.

7. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. CYCG is not responsible for the privacy practices, policies, or content of external sites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of those websites independently.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

CYCG may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in legal requirements, business practices, or service offerings. Updates will be posted on our website with the latest effective date.

9. Contact Information

For questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact CYCG through the official contact channels listed on our website.

CYCG remains committed to transparency, accountability, and responsible data stewardship, ensuring that trust underpins every engagement and interaction.
